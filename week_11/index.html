About me...

What I Do...

UX

The foundation of any good website is good user experience. A site can be beautiful on its surface but if it doesn't makes sense to users, they'll go elsewhere.

Research

Even when you think you know how a site should be designed, you can be surprised. A little targeted user research at the start of a project can save a lot of headaches later.

Design

The design of the site should reflect the content, not the other way around. I build sites from the ground up, allowing your content to lead the way.

Development

Responsive development is not just making a site accessible from any device. It's also making thoughtful decisions about what content makes sense in different contexts.
